The Code of Conduct.

To maintain an elite environment, we enforce strict guidelines.

1. Fairness Doctrine

If it's not in the vanilla client or our approved mod-list, don't use it. This includes "lightweight" macros and double-click hardware exploits.

2. Composure

High stakes bring high emotions. However, targeted harassment or hate speech will result in an permanent, non-appealable ban.

3. Manipulation

Leaderboard tampering, "throwing" matches, or exploiting map bugs to avoid combat is strictly forbidden.
