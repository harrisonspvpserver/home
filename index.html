The Project Lore.

Harrison’s PvP was born from a desire to return Minecraft combat to its roots: skill, timing, and strategy.

The Infrastructure

We host on dedicated Ryzen 9 hardware located in central hubs to ensure that your reach and hit registration are never compromised by server-side lag.

The Community

Unlike massive networks, we focus on a "tight-knit" feel. Our staff are active players who understand the nuances of high-level play and kit balancing.

Why Play Here?

  • No Pay-To-Win: Cosmetics only. We never sell kits or combat advantages.
  • Custom Maps: Arenas designed specifically to prevent camping and encourage movement.
  • Active Development: New updates and balance patches are released frequently.

Server Guidelines.

1. Modifications

Allowed: Optifine, Sodium, Lunar/Badlion (without prohibited mods).
Banned: Autoclickers, reach mods, and X-Ray.

2. Behavior

Competitive banter is fine. Personal attacks, racism, or leaking private information (doxing) results in an instant IP ban.

3. Exploits

Report bugs instead of using them. Using "block-hitting" glitches or map-breaking exploits will reset your Leaderboard standing.
